## REACT: Rational Engineering of Active Catalytic Topologies

My research focuses on the atomic-level design of zeolite active sites to solve complex environmental and energy challenges.

### 1. Atomic-level Control of Zeolite Active Sites
* Investigation of Al-siting and its influence on catalytic performance.
* Development of stable dinuclear Ti-sites (Ti-O-Ti) in TS-1 frameworks.

### 2. Sustainable Catalysis & Carbon Circularity
* **CO2 Hydrogenation:** Selective production of light hydrocarbons using hybrid catalyst platforms.
* **Propylene Epoxidation:** Efficient synthesis using in-situ generated H2O2.
* **Tandem Catalysis:** Integrating multi-functional sites for one-pot chemical transformation.
