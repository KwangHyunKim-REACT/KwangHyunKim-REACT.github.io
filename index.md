# Welcome to REACT (Rational Engineering of Active Catalytic Topologies)

I am **Kwang Hyun Kim**, a Ph.D. Candidate at **UNIST** (Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology) in the School of Energy and Chemical Engineering. My research is dedicated to solving global energy and environmental challenges through the precision engineering of catalytic materials.

## Research Focus
My doctoral work, under the supervision of **Prof. Ja Hun Kwak**, focuses on:
* **Atomic-level Design:** Precision control of zeolite active sites (e.g., Al-siting, Ti-O-Ti dinuclear sites).
* **Sustainable Conversion:** Developing high-performance catalysts for CO2 hydrogenation and propylene epoxidation.
* **Spectroscopic Analysis:** Utilizing advanced characterization to establish structure-performance relationships.
