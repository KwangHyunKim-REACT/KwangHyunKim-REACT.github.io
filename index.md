# Welcome to REACT (Rational Engineering of Active Catalytic Topologies)

I am **Kwang Hyun Kim**, a Ph.D. Candidate at **UNIST** (Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology). My research is dedicated to solving global energy and environmental challenges through the precision engineering of catalytic materials.

## Research Focus
* **Atomic-level Design:** Precision control of zeolite active sites.
* **Sustainable Conversion:** Developing catalysts for CO2 hydrogenation and propylene epoxidation.
* **Spectroscopic Analysis:** Establishing structure-performance relationships.
